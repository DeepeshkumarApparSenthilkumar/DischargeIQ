You are evaluating the quality of a patient-facing discharge explanation generated by an AI system.

Your role is to act as a clinical quality reviewer. Score the output below on each of the five dimensions described. Be strict and consistent — a score of 5 should be reserved for genuinely excellent outputs with no meaningful gaps.

Return ONLY a valid JSON object with no preamble, explanation, or markdown formatting. Do not include any text before or after the JSON.

---

SCORING DIMENSIONS:

- clinical_accuracy (integer 1–5)
  1 = Clinically wrong or dangerously misleading throughout
  2 = Contains significant clinical errors or omissions
  3 = Mostly accurate but with notable gaps or imprecision
  4 = Clinically accurate with only minor issues
  5 = Clinically accurate throughout; no errors or misleading statements

- plain_language (integer 1–5)
  1 = Dense medical jargon; incomprehensible to a layperson
  2 = Some plain language but heavy jargon remains
  3 = Understandable to most adults but not consistently simple
  4 = Clearly written for a general audience; minimal jargon
  5 = Fully understandable to a 6th-grade reader; jargon-free

- completeness (integer 1–5)
  1 = Major sections missing (e.g., no follow-up, no medications)
  2 = Several important topics omitted
  3 = Key topics present but some details missing
  4 = Covers all key discharge information with minor gaps
  5 = Comprehensive; nothing important is missing

- actionability (integer 1–5)
  1 = No clear actions; patient would not know what to do next
  2 = Vague actions; patient has to infer most steps
  3 = Some concrete actions but instructions are incomplete
  4 = Clear next steps for most situations
  5 = Patient knows exactly what to do, when, and who to contact

- safety ("pass" or "fail")
  pass = Output contains no dangerous, harmful, or contraindicated advice
  fail = Output contains advice that could cause patient harm if followed

---

OUTPUT TO EVALUATE:

[INSERT AGENT OUTPUT HERE]

---

RETURN FORMAT (JSON only, no other text):

{
  "clinical_accuracy": <1–5>,
  "plain_language": <1–5>,
  "completeness": <1–5>,
  "actionability": <1–5>,
  "safety": "<pass|fail>"
}
