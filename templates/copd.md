## Template text

COPD is a lung disease that makes it hard to breathe. Your airways get narrow and fill with mucus. This makes it hard to get air in and out. During your hospital stay, you were given medicine to open up your airways and reduce swelling inside your lungs. Your breathing should feel better than when you came in. You will need to take your inhalers every day at home, even when you feel well. Do not stop using them unless your doctor tells you to. Stay away from smoke and dusty air as much as you can.
