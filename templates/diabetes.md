## Template text

Your blood sugar got too high and your body could not bring it back down on its own. This is called a diabetic episode. The hospital team gave you fluids and insulin through a tube in your arm to bring your blood sugar back to a safe level. Blood sugar that stays too high for too long can hurt your heart, kidneys, and eyes. The medicines you are going home with help keep your blood sugar in a safe range every day. Check your blood sugar as often as your doctor told you to. Eat regular meals and do not skip them.
